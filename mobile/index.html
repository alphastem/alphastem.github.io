Building Android Apps with App Inventor (Fall 2018)

For 5th to 8th graders.

In this class, we will learn to build mobile apps with MIT's App inventor 2. App inventor 2 is very similar to Scratch. Students can build mobile apps without writing code.

Requirements

  1. Programming experience with scratch.
  2. Bring your own computer.
    3. It is recommended to bring an Android device to the class.
