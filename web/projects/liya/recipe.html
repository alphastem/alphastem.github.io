Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 24 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter, softened 1 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Packed brown sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
All-purpose flour 3 cups
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Hot water 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Semisweet chocolate chips 2 cups
Chopped Walnuts (optional) 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Step 2: Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
Step 3: Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Miss Sigg's Snickerdoodles

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 48 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter, softened 1/2 cup
Shortening 1/2 cup
White sugar 1 1/2 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
All-purpose flour 2 3/4 cups
Cream of tartar 2 teaspoons
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
White sugar 2 tablespooons
Ground cinnamon 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Step 2: Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups sugar, the eggs and the vanilla. Blend in the flour, cream of tartar, soda and salt. Shape dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls.
Step 3: Mix the 2 tablespoons sugar and the cinnamon. Roll balls of dough in mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Step 4: Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until set but not too hard. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
