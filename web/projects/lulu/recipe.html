Recipe #1

  • Time: Takes about 25-30 minutes
  • Serves: (8x8(square pan)) Brownies
Ingredients Quantity
white sugar 1/2 cup
butter 2 tablespoons
water 2 tablespoons
semisweet chocolate chips 1 1/2 cups
eggs 2
vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon
all-purpose flour 2/3
baking soda 1/4 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
Step 1:Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (or else 165 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch square pan.
Step 2:Using a medium saucepan, put in the sugar, butter and water. Cook over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth. Mix in the eggs and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the chocolate mixture. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Step 3:Bake the brownies for 25-30 minutes in the oven, until brownies set up. Don't overbake. Cool in pan and cut them into whatever you want!
