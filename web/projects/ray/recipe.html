Recipe #1: Chicken Noddle Soup

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: six people
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Boil 8 of water at about 212 Fahrenheit for about 10 minutes
Step 2: Add 1 cup of grounded chicken breast
Step 3: Slice about 5 slices of ginger each 2 inches long
Step 4: Add half cup of sliced onion
Step 5: Add half cup of cutted cabage
Step 6: Add half cup of diced carrots
Step 7: Add half cup of diced tomatos
Step 8: Serve and enjoy!

Recipe #3: Tacos

  • Time: About 17 minutes
  • Serves: one person

Step 1: Cook beef in an 10 inch skillet over medium 8-10 minutes until brown
Step 2: Stir salsa into the beef, heat to boiling and stir constantly
Step 3: Turn heat to medium-low and cook for five minutes
Step 4: Pour beef mixture into a large serving bowl
Step 5: Heat taco shells
Step 6: Serve taco shell with the beef mixture, cheese, lettuce, and tomato
Step 7: Eat and enjoy!
